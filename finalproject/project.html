Overview of Universal Design

Universal design is a concept that focuses on creating products, environments, and services that are accessible, usable, and beneficial to as many people as possible, regardless of their abilities, age, or situation. It seeks to make the world a more inclusive place by considering the diverse needs of all users from the very beginning of the design process. The core idea is to design for the broadest range of users, ensuring that no one is excluded due to physical, cognitive, or other barriers.
